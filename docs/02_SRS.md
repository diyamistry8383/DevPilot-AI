# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

The purpose of DevPilot AI is to develop an AI-powered engineering productivity platform that assists software development teams in planning, prioritizing, tracking, and analyzing engineering work.

The platform integrates with project management systems and provides AI-driven recommendations, analytics, reports, and intelligent automation.

## 1.2 Scope

DevPilot AI provides:

- Project Management
- Sprint Management
- AI Task Prioritization
- AI Assistant
- Analytics Dashboard
- Resume Generator
- Learning Recommendation
- Automated Reporting
- Multi-Agent AI
- RAG Knowledge Base
