# DevPilot AI – AI-Powered Engineering Productivity & Work Intelligence Platform

---

# 1. Project Overview

DevPilot AI is an AI-powered Engineering Productivity Platform designed to improve software development workflows by integrating project management, artificial intelligence, analytics, and developer productivity into one centralized platform.

The platform connects with Zoho Sprints and future integrations such as GitHub, Slack, Jira, Google Calendar, Microsoft Teams, and Email services.

Using Artificial Intelligence, DevPilot AI assists developers and engineering managers by recommending task priorities, generating sprint reports, predicting project risks, tracking productivity, and providing engineering insights.

Unlike traditional project management systems, DevPilot AI acts as an intelligent engineering copilot.

---

# 2. Background

Modern software teams use multiple applications such as:

- Zoho Sprints
- GitHub
- Slack
- Email
- Personal Notes
- Documentation

Developers constantly switch between these tools.

Managers spend hours creating:

- Weekly Reports
- Sprint Reports
- Team Analytics
- Productivity Reports
- Executive Summaries

This repetitive work reduces engineering productivity.

DevPilot AI automates these activities using Artificial Intelligence.
